API Information

You can get a free gemini api key to access google's ai models by signing up for google ai studio. The free tier provides generous rate limits for lightweight models (like gemini flash) without requiring a credit card, though your prompts may be used to train google's models.

Step-by-step guide

  1. Sign in: Go to google ai studio and log in with your google account.
  2. Accept terms: If it's your first time, agree to the terms of service.
  3. Create the key: Click get api key in the left sidebar, then click create api key.
  4. Copy and save: Copy the generated key and store it securely. Do not commit it to public repositories.

Limitations of the free tier

  • Rate limits: The free plan restricts how many requests you can make per minute.
  • Data usage: Your prompt and response data may be reviewed by human reviewers and used to improve google products.

App Password

To generate an app password for your gmail account, you must have 2-step verification turned on. Once enabled, you can create a unique, 16-digit password for a third-party app or device by following these steps:

  1. Go to your google account app passwords page.
  2. If prompted, sign in to your google account.
  3. At the bottom of the page, click select app and choose the app you are using, or select other to type a custom name (e.g., "Social Media Assistant").
  4. Click create. A 16-digit app password will appear on the screen. Copy it.
